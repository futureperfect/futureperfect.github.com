About

I'm a software engineer in Atlanta, Georgia. My interests are mobile, machine learning, and human-computer interaction. I spent three years working for a VoIP startup and learned a lot about how hard it is making reliable software.

I graduated from Georgia Tech with degrees in Chemistry and Computer Science. In between I worked as a researcher at PulseNet in the Centers for Disease Control.

For fun I like to race track bikes.
